Data Visualisation at Befittd

The Internship

I interned at Befittd over the Summer of 2014. Befittd is a London based startup that provides an online sizing tool for high-end fashion retailers. To learn more about Befittd, check out the Paul Smith store and click on Size Advice.

After my two month internship I was hired by befittd to take my project further, and I worked with them until the start of the new academic year.

Whilst at Befittd, I worked on a data visualisation project.

The Problem

We collect a lot of data, from each sale, from each page load and mouse click. It is hard to make sense of this data by simply looking at mySQL query results.

We needed something that our non-technical customers could understand and something that could convince potential customers to take us on board. Quick, clear and interactive visualisations that channel meaning at a glance.

The obvious solution was to take all the data we had, and stick it in a graph: that's how the Analytics Portal was born, and it is currently used by Befittd for internal and customer feedback. The portal is designed to give a customised and tailored experience for each customer we invite. For instance, representatives from our customer Stella McCartney are shown data relative to sales and user growth on their store only.
